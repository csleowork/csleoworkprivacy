We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using our application.

1. Definitions

To help you better understand this Privacy Policy, we define the following key terms:

  • "Personal Data": Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual, including but not limited to name, email address, device identifiers, and location data.
  • "We", "Our", "Us": Refers to the company or entity that owns and operates the application.
  • "You", "User": Refers to the individual or entity that uses or interacts with the application.
  • "Third Party": Refers to external organizations or services, such as advertising partners, that we may work with or that may collect data through our application.
  • "Advertising Partners": Companies that require us to integrate their code in order to provide advertising services in our application, which may lead to serving advertisements that are most relevant to you.
  • "Data Processing Partners": Companies that help us process, store, or manage the data we collect, including personal data, for purposes such as analytics, cloud hosting, or improving our service delivery.

2. Information We Collect

We do not actively collect or store personal data. However, our application may collect certain information through integrated advertising services (such as AdMob), which are typically used for ad serving, analysis, and optimization. This may includes:

  • IP Address: Used to determine the user's geographical location and serve region-specific ad content.
  • Browser Type and Version: Helps advertisers optimize the way ads are displayed to ensure compatibility across different devices and browsers.
  • Operating System Information: Includes the type and version of the operating system (e.g., Android, iOS).
  • Ad IDs: Used to track ad performance, especially in cross-device ad tracking, to provide targeted ads.
  • Device Information: Includes device type, manufacturer, screen resolution, language, etc.
  • App Usage Data: Activity data such as ad click-through rates, time spent on ads, and interaction behaviors.
  • User Behavioral Data: Interactions with ads (clicks, scrolls, frequency of ad closing).
  • Purchase or Conversion Behavior: Tracks if a user made a purchase or performed another action after viewing an ad.
  • Web Activity Data: Information about the websites users visit, collected through cookies, tracking pixels, and similar technologies, used to create user interest profiles for personalized ad targeting.

3. How We Use the Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and optimize advertising services.
  • Analyze and improve the user experience of the app.
  • Serve personalized ad content.
  • Monitor and analyze the performance of ads.

6. User Rights

We respect users’ privacy rights and are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws. Depending on your region, you may have specific rights related to your personal data. Below are the privacy rights applicable to certain users and how to exercise these rights.

Since we do not directly collect or process your personal data, all data collection and processing are done by advertising and data processing partners. If you wish to exercise your rights, particularly requests for deletion, modification, or access, please contact the relevant advertising and data processing partners directly using their privacy management tools.

6.1 Rights under the EU GDPR and EPD (European Data Protection)

If you are a resident of the European Union (EU) or the UK, under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can request access to the personal data we process about you.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request the correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request that your personal data be transferred to another service provider.
  • Right to be Informed: You can request to know the types of data we collect, how it is used, and how it is processed.

6.2 Rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know the categories of personal data we collect and the purposes for which it is collected.
  • Right to Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we have collected about you.
  • Right to Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal data for advertising and other purposes.
  • Right to Limit Processing: You have the right to limit the processing of sensitive personal information (e.g., race, gender).

6.3 Rights under the Colorado Privacy Act (CPA)

  • Right to Know: You can request details regarding the categories and purposes of personal data collected.
  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized advertising or data sales.

6.4 Rights under the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA)

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized advertising.

6.1 Rights under the EU GDPR and EPD (European Data Protection)

If you are a resident of the European Union (EU) or the UK, under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can request access to the personal data we process about you.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request the correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request that your personal data be transferred to another service provider.
  • Right to be Informed: You can request to know the types of data we collect, how it is used, and how it is processed.

6.2 Rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know the categories of personal data we collect and the purposes for which it is collected.
  • Right to Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we have collected about you.
  • Right to Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal data for advertising and other purposes.
  • Right to Limit Processing: You have the right to limit the processing of sensitive personal information (e.g., race, gender).

6.3 Rights under the Colorado Privacy Act (CPA)

  • Right to Know: You can request details regarding the categories and purposes of personal data collected.
  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized advertising or data sales.

6.4 Rights under the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA)
